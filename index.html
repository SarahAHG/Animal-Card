Red-eared slider

It is an aquatic turtle, a strong swimmer, and in the wild, will commonly be seen basking on rocks, logs, or other surfaces above the water.

  • Scientific Name: Chrysemys scripta elegans.
  • Average Length: 12 inches.
  • Average Lifespan: 50-70 years.
  • Habitat: Swamps.

Turtles are reptiles, and cold-blooded, so they must rely on external heat sources for warmth. They will bask in sunlight, and in the wild, burrow down into the earth to hibernate in winter. The three main concerns in keeping a Red-eared Slider healthy are warmth, clean water, and proper diet.
